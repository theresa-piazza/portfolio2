Hi! I'm Theresa Piazza

I'm studying software engineering in Fort Greene, Brooklyn. I enjoy designing beautiful, accessible websites and creating powerful code that combines to produce a holistic & seamless web experience for clients.


I previously worked in the architecture field, and I bring that attention to detail, appreciation for design, and client-focused sensibility to my new career as a web developer.

about me

I'm passionate about software development, and get so much satisfaction out of learning new techniques to streamline my coding and make it more effective and sophisticated. I want to offer clients the ability to take their amorphous ideas and create something beautiful with clean and validated code, that navigates intuitively and utilizes all possible strategies for search engine optimization.


I'm proficient in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and am beginning my study of Python, Ruby, and SQL. I want to improve my skills and learn all I can by working with a team of great developers. My other interests include cycling, reading novels, and traveling as much as possible.
